the kakeibo method

The same four questions, since 1904.

1

How much do you have?

Allowance, part-time paycheck, birthday cash — tell the bot what's actually coming in.

2

How much do you want to save?

Pick a number, even a small one. The bot works backward from it instead of guessing for you.

3

How much are you spending?

Tell it what went out — delivery, subscriptions, impulse buys — and it sorts the chaos into categories.

4

How can you improve?

The bot points out one honest, doable change instead of a full life overhaul you won't stick to.
